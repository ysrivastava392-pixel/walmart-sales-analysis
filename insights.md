# Key Insights from Walmart Sales Analysis

## 1. Monthly Sales Pattern
Sales show clear seasonal trends. Mid-year months generally record higher sales compared to the beginning of the year, indicating seasonal demand fluctuations.

## 2. Strong Store Performance Differences
Sales contribution varies significantly across stores. A small number of stores contribute a large portion of total revenue, highlighting opportunities for performance benchmarking.

## 3. Holiday vs Non-Holiday Sales
Non-holiday weeks account for the majority of total sales due to their higher frequency across the year. However, holiday weeks still show noticeable sales spikes on a per-week basis.

## 4. Importance of Data Cleaning
Removing duplicates and validating missing values ensured that the analysis results were reliable and not skewed by data quality issues.

## 5. Business Implication
Understanding seasonal trends and store-level performance can help management optimize inventory planning, staffing, and promotional strategies.

## 6. Scope for Future Analysis
Future work could include:
- Store-wise holiday impact comparison
- Forecasting weekly sales using historical trends
- Analyzing the effect of economic factors like CPI and unemployment on sales.
